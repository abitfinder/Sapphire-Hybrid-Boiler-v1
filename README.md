## 🌟 Overview
This project introduces a revolutionary **Hybrid Supercritical Steam Generation System**. By integrating industrial-grade **Sapphire Optics** with **Al-Si Metallic Phase Change Materials (PCM)**, this boiler achieves extreme thermal density and a storage capacity of up to **48 hours** without external energy input.

The system utilizes dual-energy harvesting: **Concentrated Solar Flux** and **Precision Laser Injection** to achieve instantaneous steam phase transitions.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Sapphire Core:** High-pressure resistant monocrystalline sapphire tubes for optical transparency and thermal stability.
* **Dual-Source Heating:** Integrated ports for Solar and Laser (Nd:YAG 1.06µm) heating.
* **Al-Si Thermal Storage:** Eutectic Aluminum-Silicon alloy matrix for latent heat storage (577°C melting point).
* **95% Thermal Efficiency:** Advanced regenerative water jacket to capture stray thermal radiation.
* **Sustainable & Open:** Designed to be replicated and improved by the global engineering community.
